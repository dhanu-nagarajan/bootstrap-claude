# Recovery Protocol

> Follow these steps after context compaction. Under 50 lines by design.

## When You Feel Lost or Rules Seem Missing

### Step 1: STOP
Do not continue working. Do not guess at constraints.

### Step 2: Re-read invariants
Read `.claude/shield/invariants.md` — these are the non-negotiable rules.

### Step 3: Re-read session state
Read `.claude/shield/session-state.md` — this is what you were doing.

### Step 4: Re-read CLAUDE.md
Read `CLAUDE.md` at repository root — this is the full project context.

### Step 5: Verify understanding
State back to the user:
- Current task (from session-state.md)
- Current approach (from session-state.md)
- Key constraints that apply (from invariants.md)

### Step 6: Resume
Continue from the last completed step in session-state.md.

---

**Do not skip steps. Do not paraphrase from memory. Re-read the actual files.**
